Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  • Urad Dal
  • Kidney Beans
  • Butter
  • Cream

Recipe

  1. Soak urad dal and rajma overnight, then pressure cook with water.
  2. Heat butter and oil, sauté cumin, onions, ginger-garlic paste, and tomato puree.
  3. Cook until oil separates, add chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  4. Add cooked dal and rajma, simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add cream and butter, cook for 5 minutes, and serve hot.

White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Pasta

Recipe

  1. Boil pasta and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic and veggies in butter.
  3. Melt butter in another pan, add flour, stir.
  4. Add milk gradually, stir until thick, season.
  5. Mix pasta and veggies, stir, and serve hot.

Siddu

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Yeast
  • Walnuts
  • Urad Dal

Recipe

  1. Prepare dough with wheat flour, water, salt; rest 1 hour.
  2. Make stuffing with mashed potatoes, spices, chilies.
  3. Stuff dough discs and seal like dumplings.
  4. Steam for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve with ghee or chutney.

Biryani

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes

Recipe

  1. Marinate with yogurt, spices, and rest.
  2. Partially cook rice with whole spices.
  3. Layer rice and marinated mix with herbs and saffron milk.
  4. Seal and cook on dum for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve with raita.

Dal Baati

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Semolina
  • Yogurt
  • Spices

Recipe

  1. Make baati dough and roast until golden.
  2. Cook toor, moong, chana dal with spices.
  3. Temper dal with ghee, mustard, garlic.
  4. Dip baatis in ghee, crush lightly.
  5. Serve with dal and chutney.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Tomato Puree
  • Butter
  • Cream

Recipe

  1. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and spices in butter.
  2. Blend into a smooth puree.
  3. Add paneer cubes and cream.
  4. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan.
